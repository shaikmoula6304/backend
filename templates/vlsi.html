What is VLSI? A Beginner’s Guide

By Shaik Moulali • April 9, 2025

🔍 Introduction

VLSI stands for Very Large Scale Integration, and it’s the process of creating integrated circuits by combining thousands or millions of transistors on a single chip...

💡 Why is VLSI Important?

  • Used in smartphones, laptops, IoT devices
  • Enables miniaturization of electronics
  • Vital for ECE students aiming for core jobs

🛠️ Skills You Need

To start with VLSI, learn Verilog, Digital Electronics, CMOS design, and tools like Cadence or Synopsys...
